GloryBer's Spanish Academy

Alexandria, VA • Est. 2021 • Bilingual Daycare

Visit Site ↗

GloryBer's Spanish Academy is a nurturing bilingual daycare in Alexandria, VA helping little ones learn and thrive in both English and Spanish since 2021. TM Marketing & Co delivered a full digital overhaul.

Website RedesignBrand IdentityUX/UI DesignSEOContent StrategyBilingual EN/ES

The academy had a compelling mission but an outdated online presence that didn't reflect their warmth, credibility, or bilingual identity.

  • Outdated design not reflecting brand personality
  • Poor mobile experience losing prospective families
  • No clear enrollment pathway or calls to action
  • English-only content missing bilingual families

We built a fully custom website — vibrant, welcoming, and built for conversions — with bilingual content and a clear enrollment flow.

  • Custom responsive design with on-brand visuals
  • Full EN/ES bilingual toggle throughout
  • Optimized program pages with enrollment CTAs
  • SEO-ready structure for local search visibility

The new site launched to immediate positive feedback with a cleaner, more professional presence that builds trust at first glance.

Associates

Washington, DC • International Economic Development

D.O Clark & Associates is a bespoke international economic development and advisory firm specializing in cross-border trade, investment facilitation, and agribusiness. TM Marketing & Co built their digital presence from the ground up — a sophisticated, globally-positioned website to match their world-class advisory work.

Website Design & Dev Brand Identity Content Strategy SEO Copywriting UX/UI Design

D.O Clark & Associates needed a digital presence that reflected the prestige and complexity of their international advisory work — without a single existing web asset to start from.

  • No existing website or digital footprint
  • Needed to convey trust and expertise to high-level international partners
  • Complex multi-service offering requiring clear, compelling structure
  • Brand voice needed to balance gravitas with accessibility

We built a full multi-page website with a premium, editorial aesthetic — dark, refined, and globally authoritative. Every page was crafted to position D.O Clark & Associates as a world-class advisory firm.

  • Custom multi-page site with cinematic hero and animations
  • Strategic content architecture across 5 pages
  • Full brand identity including color system and typography
  • SEO-optimized structure and metadata throughout

D.O Clark & Associates launched with a polished, professional online presence that immediately signals credibility to international partners, investors, and institutions.
