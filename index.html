GloryBer's Spanish
Academy

Alexandria, VA  •  Est. 2021  •  Bilingual Daycare

Visit Site ↗

GloryBer's Spanish Academy is a nurturing bilingual daycare in Alexandria, VA helping little ones learn, grow, and thrive in both English and Spanish since 2021. TM Marketing & Co delivered a full digital overhaul to match their vibrant energy and heart-first approach.

Website RedesignBrand IdentityUX/UI DesignSEOContent StrategyBilingual EN/ES

The academy had a compelling mission but an outdated online presence that didn't reflect their warmth, credibility, or bilingual identity. Parents couldn't easily find program information or contact the team.

  • Outdated design not reflecting brand personality
  • Poor mobile experience losing prospective families
  • No clear enrollment pathway or calls to action
  • English-only content missing bilingual families

We built a fully custom website from the ground up — vibrant, welcoming, and built for conversions — with bilingual content, streamlined program pages, and a clear enrollment flow.

  • Custom responsive design with playful, on-brand visuals
  • Full EN/ES bilingual toggle throughout
  • Optimized program pages with enrollment CTAs
  • SEO-ready structure for local search visibility

The new site launched to immediate positive feedback with a cleaner, more professional presence that builds trust with prospective families at first glance.
